11/4/24 | Gunpla

As an introduction I've never really been too into the actual media that these figures are based upon. I basically stumbled into the hobby on accident on a trip to South Korea when I walked into a Gundam pop-up store. I built my first Gundam and ever since I've been drilling an ever widening hole in my wallet to buy more. I'm not sure I could really explain to someone what is objectively so fun about making these. To me there's something so special about spending hours and hours meticulously putting in every small detail on these figures, but it's back breaking and stressful work (especially for someone with somewhat perfectionist tendencies). It doesn't help that the more I get into the hobby the more detail I want to put into the figures leading to me spending more and more time on the figures and more money on special tools. My most recent build (the RG Freedom Gundam) took around 16 hours of work but I'm very proud of the result.
